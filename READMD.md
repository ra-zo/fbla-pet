## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Enter your pet's name, choose a type, and set your starting budget
3. Click "Create Pet" to begin
4. Take care of your pet by feeding, playing, and keeping them healthy
5. Earn money through chores to afford pet care
6. Set savings goals and manage your budget wisely
7. Watch your pet's mood change based on how well you care for them!
